Everything a hospital needs to scale patient follow-up

From health camp data ingestion to structured FHIR records — fully automated, multilingual, and compliant.

Indic Voice AI Follow-ups

Automated outbound voice calls in Hindi, English, Bhojpuri, and Maithili — with natural Hindi+English code-switching. Powered by Gemini 2.0 Flash native audio (STT + LLM + TTS in one model). Runs in-browser via LiveKit WebRTC for demo; production-ready via SIP/Twilio telephony bridge.

Automatic FHIR R4 / ABDM Records

Every voice interaction auto-generates ABDM-compliant FHIR R4 resources — OPD Consultation Notes, Diagnostic Reports, Prescription Bundles, AllergyIntolerance records — coded with SNOMED CT and LOINC. Indian brand-name drugs (e.g., Crocin) are automatically mapped to INN + SNOMED codes.

3-Level Triage & Escalation

Voice AI detects severity in real time. Level 1 (critical) immediately guides the patient to call 108 or go to the ER and fires all alerts. Level 2 sends an SMS + Supabase Realtime dashboard alert to the care coordinator within one hour. Level 3 logs and queues for the next scheduled follow-up.

Human-in-the-Loop Doctor Review

AI-extracted symptoms, medications, and FHIR resources land in the doctor's review queue. Doctors Approve, Correct, or Reject each extraction. Corrections are stored and shown in the admin accuracy dashboard — creating a feedback loop that improves the AI over time.

DPDP Act 2023 & ABDM Consent

Full 5-step ABDM consent flow. Verbal consent recorded and timestamped before every call. Patients can view, revoke, or request deletion of their data from the patient portal. Granular consent management and audit logs for every data access event.

Camp-Wise Analytics & Workflow Builder

Admin dashboard shows risk distribution, camp-wise breakdowns (Gandhi Maidan, Digha Slum, Aashrya Old Age Home, Disha Deaddiction Center), and AI accuracy metrics. Build and manage follow-up workflows (Screening→OPD, Post-Discharge, Chronic Monthly) without writing code.

From health camp data to structured clinical records — automatically

01

Ingest Health Camp Data

Upload CSV data from any health camp. Risk scores (cardiac, diabetic, hypertension) are auto-computed. HIGH-risk patients are queued for immediate voice follow-up; MODERATE for 48-hour call; LOW for health-tip reminder.

02

AI Places a Voice Call in the Patient's Language

Gemini 2.0 Flash handles listening, understanding, and responding — all natively in one model. No separate STT/TTS pipeline needed. The AI adapts tone and speed — slower and empathetic for elderly patients at Aashrya Old Age Home, direct and simplified for Digha Slum residents.

03

Structured Extraction & Triage

Every symptom, medication, reading, and allergy is extracted as a FHIR R4 resource with SNOMED/LOINC codes. Severity is assessed in real time. If escalation is needed, the care coordinator's dashboard lights up via Supabase Realtime — no polling, no delay.

04

Doctor Reviews & Approves

AI extractions appear in the doctor's queue with the full call transcript. One click to Approve confirms the FHIR record. Correct lets the doctor fix a wrong extraction (e.g., headache → migraine with the right SNOMED code). Reject discards a bad extraction. All actions feed the improvement loop.

05

Patient Sees Their Own Health Record

The patient's portal shows vitals, recent call summaries, upcoming scheduled calls, and FHIR-linked health reports. They can manage DPDP consent, revoke data sharing, or download their records — aligned with the ABDM PHR standard.
