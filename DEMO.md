# Swadhikaar Demo Guide (5 Minutes, Demo-First)

This guide is optimized for a live main-screen presentation where the demo is the primary impact driver.

## 5-Minute Live Script (Word-for-Word)

### 0:00-0:20 — Hook

"Good morning/afternoon, I am Ace, and this is Swadhikaar."

"In SME hospitals, one doctor handles thousands of patients, so follow-up calls often do not happen."

"In the next 5 minutes, I will show a live flow: patient voice interaction to doctor escalation in real time."

### 0:20-0:40 — Problem + Promise

"Swadhikaar is an Indic voice AI patient engagement platform."

"It calls patients in Hindi/English, extracts clinical signals, triages risk, and pushes urgent cases to the care team dashboard."

### 0:40-1:10 — Login and Role Setup

"This is role-based: patient, coordinator/doctor, and admin."

"I will use pre-configured demo accounts and show one end-to-end journey."

### 1:10-2:00 — Patient View

Log in as patient: `patient@swadhikaar.in` / `patient123`

"The patient sees health overview, call history, consent controls, and the voice assistant."

"This matters for low-literacy and elderly users where text-first systems fail."

"Now I will trigger a follow-up voice interaction."

### 2:00-3:00 — Voice to Extraction to Triage

"Now the AI captures symptoms from natural conversation and converts unstructured speech into structured clinical signals."

Say a short symptom sample:

"I have dizziness and headache since 3 days."

"Based on symptom plus risk context, triage marks severity from LOW to CRITICAL."

### 3:00-4:00 — Doctor/Coordinator Impact

Log in or switch to coordinator/doctor: `coordinator@swadhikaar.in` / `coordinator123`

"Here is the high-impact moment: escalation appears for care team action."

"The doctor can review AI output with transcript and clinical context."

"This is human-in-the-loop. AI assists, doctor decides."

### 4:00-4:35 — Admin and Scale Signal

Switch to admin: `admin@swadhikaar.in` / `admin123`

"Admin can monitor operations, workflow coverage, and consent/compliance posture."

"The same platform handles multiple care workflows, including follow-ups and newborn vaccination tracking."

### 4:35-5:00 — Closing

"Swadhikaar turns follow-up from a staffing bottleneck into a software workflow."

"Voice-first engagement plus real-time triage plus human clinical review means better coverage at lower cost."

"Thank you."

## Pre-Demo Setup (Open Before Going On Stage)

- Window 1: patient dashboard/calls
- Window 2: doctor escalations/review
- Window 3: admin dashboard/workflows
- Browser zoom: `125%` to `150%`
- Close extra tabs and disable notifications
- Keep credentials ready in a local note

## Backup Plan (If Live Voice Fails)

Use this line confidently:

"Network/audio can vary in live halls, so I will continue with recorded or seeded workflow state."

Then show this sequence:

1. Existing call history
2. Escalation queue
3. Doctor review screen
4. Admin metrics/workflows

This still demonstrates end-to-end product impact.
